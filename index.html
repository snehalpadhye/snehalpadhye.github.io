About

Hello! I am Snehal Padhye.

PhD Candidate, Imaging Science, RIT

I am a third year PhD student at Chester F Carlson Center for Imaging Science at Rochester Institute of Technology, New York. I am priviledged to have Dr. James Ferwerda as my advisor. I am working in Visual Imaging and Technology Lab in developing methods for realistic digital representation of cultural heritage objects. It involves techniques to capture and model surface topography and material reflectance/transmission of complex planar objects like paintings and manusripts. This has given me an opportunity to work on topics as diverse as camera calibation, color caibration, structured light and writing cool graphics applications.
